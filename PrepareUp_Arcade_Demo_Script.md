# PrepareUp — Arcade Demo Script & Direction
**Format:** Clean Walkthrough · 2–2.5 min  
**Audience:** Recruiters, Industry Professionals, Teachers  
**Tool:** Arcade (arcade.software)  
**Goal:** Show the full loop — upload a document → study assets generated → voice learning — in one smooth, narrated flow.

---

## Pre-Production Checklist

Before recording in Arcade, have these ready:

- [ ] A real PDF or document loaded (suggested: a 3–5 page industry report, lecture notes, or HR policy doc — something your target audience recognizes as "their kind of content")
- [ ] A logged-in account so saved chats are visible
- [ ] Browser window set to **1440 × 900** — clean, no personal bookmarks bar
- [ ] Disable browser notifications
- [ ] Pre-type your chat questions so you can paste them quickly (no visible typing delays)
- [ ] Arcade Chrome Extension installed and active

## Scene-by-Scene Breakdown

---

### SCENE 1 — Landing Page Hook
**Duration:** ~15 seconds  
**What to show:** Hero section of the landing page  
**Arcade action:** Static capture, no scrolling yet

**Voiceover:**
> "Most people study by re-reading the same pages — and forget most of it within a day. PrepareUp is an AI study platform built for professionals, educators, and anyone who learns from documents. Upload your material. Get a tutor, flashcards, a quiz, and a podcast — instantly, all grounded in what you actually uploaded."

**Arcade annotation (text callout on hero):**
> "No hallucinations. Every answer tied to your content."

**Direction note:** Pause 1 second on the hero headline before the voiceover starts. Let the design breathe.

---

### SCENE 2 — Upload a Document
**Duration:** ~20 seconds  
**What to show:** The file upload area → file picker → file accepted state  
**Arcade hotspot:** Place a pulsing hotspot on the upload button

**Voiceover:**
> "Start by uploading any document — a PDF, a report, a textbook chapter. PrepareUp reads it, indexes it, and uses it as the source of truth for everything that follows."

**Arcade annotations:**
- On file picker opening → callout: `"PDF, Word, plain text — all supported"`
- On upload success state → callout: `"Processed in seconds"`

**Direction note:** Use a recognizable, professional-looking PDF — e.g. "Q1 Industry Trends Report" or "Employee Onboarding Guide." Avoid using a personal file name.

---

### SCENE 3 — Chat With Your Document
**Duration:** ~30 seconds  
**What to show:** Chat interface, send 2 questions, show grounded AI responses  
**Arcade hotspot:** Pulsing dot on the message input

**Pre-typed questions to paste in:**
1. `"What are the three main takeaways from this document?"`
2. `"Explain the second section as if I'm new to this topic."`

**Voiceover:**
> "The AI tutor has read your document. Ask it anything — a summary, a clarification, a deep-dive. Every response is pulled directly from what you uploaded, not from the internet or the model's general training."

**Arcade annotations:**
- On AI response → callout: `"Answers only from your document"`
- Highlight the response area → callout: `"No hallucinations. No generic AI filler."`

**Direction note:** After pasting the second question, pause briefly so the AI response is visible before the next scene. Do not scroll — let the conversation feel natural.

---

### SCENE 4 — Generate Flashcards
**Duration:** ~20 seconds  
**What to show:** Click "Flashcards" / generate option → flashcard view rendering  
**Arcade hotspot:** Button or sidebar option for Flashcards

**Voiceover:**
> "One click generates a full set of flashcards — terms, definitions, and concepts — all extracted from your document. Perfect for pre-meeting prep, exam revision, or onboarding new team members."

**Arcade annotations:**
- On flashcard grid appearing → callout: `"Auto-generated from your upload"`
- On a single card → callout: `"Front: key term. Back: explanation in plain English."`

**Direction note:** Show at least 3–4 cards. If the UI allows flipping a card, flip one to show the back.

---

### SCENE 5 — Mock Quiz
**Duration:** ~20 seconds  
**What to show:** Quiz generation → first question rendered → answer one question  
**Arcade hotspot:** Quiz generator button

**Voiceover:**
> "PrepareUp also builds a mock quiz from your content. Use it to test yourself, prep candidates, or check comprehension after a training session. The questions are contextual — not generic — because they come from your document."

**Arcade annotations:**
- On quiz question appearing → callout: `"Multiple choice, grounded in your content"`
- After answering → callout: `"Instant feedback with explanations"`

**Direction note:** Answer one question correctly to show the positive feedback state. Keep the pace brisk here — this is a proof point, not a tutorial.

---

### SCENE 6 — Study Guide
**Duration:** ~15 seconds  
**What to show:** Study guide generation → rendered guide with headings and summaries

**Voiceover:**
> "Need a condensed reference? The study guide gives you a structured overview — key themes, definitions, and chapter summaries — ready to save, share, or review before an important meeting or class."

**Arcade annotation:**
- On study guide → callout: `"Structured, scannable, shareable"`

---

### SCENE 7 — Voice Learning Mode (Globe)
**Duration:** ~25 seconds  
**What to show:** Navigate to Voice Learning → globe appears → click a country → select a language → start session → AI tutor speaks

**Voiceover:**
> "And then there's Voice Mode. This is PrepareUp's most immersive feature. Select a country, choose a language, and start a live voice conversation with an AI tutor — who has read your document — in that language. Whether you're preparing for an international presentation, teaching multilingual students, or just learning a new language through your own material, this closes the loop."

**Arcade annotations:**
- On globe → callout: `"63 countries. Dozens of languages."`
- On language selection panel → callout: `"Click any country to see available languages"`
- On session start → callout: `"AI tutor + your document + your language of choice"`

**Direction note:** This is the most visually striking scene. Let the globe sit for 2–3 seconds before clicking. Select a non-English language (e.g. French or Spanish) for impact. Show the AI's first response in the selected language.

---

### SCENE 8 — Saved Chats & Account
**Duration:** ~10 seconds  
**What to show:** Sidebar or chat history showing previous sessions saved to the account

**Voiceover:**
> "Every chat, every session — saved to your account and ready to pick up where you left off."

**Arcade annotation:**
- On chat list → callout: `"Sessions tied to your document. Always there when you need them."`

---

### SCENE 9 — Closing Frame
**Duration:** ~10 seconds  
**What to show:** Return to landing page hero or a clean dashboard state

**Voiceover:**
> "PrepareUp. Upload your content. Learn it faster. In any language."

**Arcade action:** End with a CTA hotspot on the sign-up or "Get Started" button.

**Arcade annotation (final callout):**
> "Free to start. No credit card."  
*(adjust based on your actual pricing)*

## Arcade Production Settings

| Setting | Recommended Value |
|---|---|
| Voiceover | Record in Arcade directly, or import MP3 per scene |
| Hotspot style | Pulsing ring — use sparingly (max 1 per scene) |
| Annotations | Tooltip bubbles, top or bottom-right placement |
| Background blur | Off — show the full UI |
| Transition | Fade (0.3s) between scenes |
| Auto-advance | Off — viewer controls pace |
| CTA button | "Try PrepareUp Free" → your signup URL |
| Cover thumbnail | The globe scene or the flashcard grid (most visual) |

---

## Voiceover Tone Notes

- **Pace:** Slightly slower than you think you need. Professionals absorb UI and narration simultaneously — give them space.
- **Tone:** Confident, direct, zero hype words ("revolutionary," "game-changer"). Let the features speak.
- **Length:** Each scene's voiceover should finish *just before* you move to the next hotspot click. Never rush a click.
- **Recording tip:** Record the full script as one take first, then trim per-scene in Arcade. This keeps pacing natural.
